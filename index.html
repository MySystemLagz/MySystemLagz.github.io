I've created quite a few games now, these include Zero Aim, Lazer Bullet, and Micro Sentry which you can find in the games tab. My favorite games are The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, Splatoon 2, Orwell, and Journey. I liked The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild because of it's adventure. The graphics are great, espically the particle effects. I liked Splatoon 2 because of it's face paced nature, making you think on your feet as you try to defeat the enemy team. I like Orwell because of it's story and how it's up to you to reveal that story.
